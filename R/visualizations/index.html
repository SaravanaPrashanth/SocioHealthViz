Project Overview

About

SocioHealthViz explores the relationship between social factors and health outcomes through interactive visualizations and comprehensive analysis.

Objectives

Our main goal is to understand and visualize the correlation between life expectancy patterns and deprivation indices across different regions.

Methods

Using R and various data visualization techniques, we analyze demographic data and social indicators to create meaningful insights.
